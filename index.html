About this project

An interactive map of New York City's street trees, built from official forestry data and styled as an architectural site plan.

  • Zoom and pan to explore — species icons appear as you zoom in.
  • Filter by borough using the top menu.
  • Toggle species with the colored chips below.
  • Turn map layers (parks, water, buildings) on or off.
  • Switch to 3D for an isometric view.
  • Click any tree to open Street View and see its details.
